something about us

We are Smart, a stationery brand that believes in, and encourages your innate creativity. To express
yourself, to work out your smallest ideas even if they begin with a single sentence, or the most
basic doodle. Smart is you, always restless, never settling. Smart enables all of us, thinkers and
doers of the world, to start on a fresh page, to create a brave new world.
